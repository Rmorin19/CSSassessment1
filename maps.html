There are 7 maps in Valorant that are based off of real places such as Santa Fe, Tokyo, and Venice. They all have different advantages for each type and induvidual character. They are all based in the near future where there has been a meteor with rock that gave people special powers.

Ascent

Ascent is placed in Venice Italy in the near future which is also where the practice range is located. Its 2 sites are played very differently from each other. One gives the defenders the high ground and has a lower room under the defenders side which is used quick frequently to help the other team hide and have shelter from the defenders. The second site gives the defenders the low ground and the attacker the advantage.


Bind

Bind is based in Morocco and has teloporters that brind you from one site to mid and one site to the other. The first site has high ground for the deffenders and the other site gives the other side the high ground. It is a very open map that favours long range pew pews and agents like Chamber and Brimstone.


Breeze

Breeze is an island in the Bermuda Triangle with ancient ruins. The map looks like a beach. The first site has 2 big pyramids in the middle and the other team has a cave while the deffenders use the ruins. The second site gives both teams the ruins and it has a big pylon in the middle.


Fracture

Fracture is where a sience expirament went wrong and kill all the scientist who where working there. This map has ziplines that let you get from one side to the other. The deffenders spawn point is in th middle of the map and has the other team spawning on eather side of them. One site is like a fortified base where as the other has a little roof you can go on to get high ground.


Haven

Haven is the only map with 3 sites. It is based in Bhutan. it has a very tradition chinese style and many cherry blossoms. The fist site gives the defenders the high ground and the other team the very low ground. The second site has many boxes and gives the other team a way to flank the defenders. The 3rd site has a lond hallway that gives the defenders the advantage but does pose more of a threat that they will get flanked.


Icebox

Ice Box is based on an island it the northen Russian sea. It is a shiping dock and the first site is base around them. The 2nd one is the place where they process the shipments. It favours the mid ranged pew pews and agents like Omen and Jett.


Split

Split is located in Tokyo, Japan and has a modern but overgrown look. Its two sites both offer high ground for the deffenders but it is very easy to counter with the other teams very good access to mid which allows for an easy flank.
