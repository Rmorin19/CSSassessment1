Valorant is a fps game developed 2020 in by Riot Games who have also made games such League of Legends, Legends of Runeterra, and Teamfight Tactics. Valorant currently has 18 agents with more in the works. Every agent has different abilitiess such as healing, running faster, poison, and fire.
